About Me

Building the future,
one circuit at a time.

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and software. With hands-on experience in embedded systems, web development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to every engineering challenge.

My capstone project, ChickMate, involved designing an AI-integrated chick brooder that combined computer vision, bioacoustics, and fuzzy logic climate control — an experience that solidified my love for building intelligent, real-world systems. I also led the development of QuakeTrace, an IoT platform that won Champion at the DOST-MIRDC competition.
